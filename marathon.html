Marathon Event

Indian Marathon is an annual international marathon event organized in India. It is a road running event that is held every year . Indian Marathon is considered to be the hottest marathon and half marathon in India.[1][2] The event was established in 2017 by Indian Runners, an umbrella brand of running events across India.[3]
